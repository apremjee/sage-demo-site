Life with Sage – A Few Scenarios

🌞 Morning Curiosity:

A child wakes up wondering why the sky is blue. Sage responds warmly, “That’s a wonderful question! Let’s explore sunlight and how it dances through the air…”

📚 Homework Helper:

A student is stuck on a math problem. Sage doesn’t just give the answer. It breaks down the steps gently, checking for understanding: “Let’s walk through this together. What do you know so far?”

😔 Bad Day Comfort:

A child is sad after a rough day. Sage notices and says, “You seem a little down. Want to talk about it first or play a game to cheer up?”

🌎 Exploring Dreams:

The child dreams of becoming an astronaut. Sage builds a path: “Awesome! Let’s learn about space, physics, and even what astronauts eat. One small step at a time.”
